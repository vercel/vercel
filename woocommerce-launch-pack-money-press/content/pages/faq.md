# Money Press — FAQ

---

## Sizing

**Q: How do your hoodies and tees fit?**

All Money Press apparel is cut oversized. The Heavyweight Hoodie and Stack Tee are designed with a relaxed, boxy silhouette. If you prefer a fitted look, size down one. If you like a very oversized drop, stay true to size or size up. Size charts with measurements are available on each product page.

**Q: Do you offer extended sizes?**

Yes. All apparel styles are available in S through XXL. If your size is sold out, use the "Notify Me" feature on the product page and we'll email you when it restocks.

## Shipping

**Q: How long does it take for my order to ship?**

Physical orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days after processing. Expedited options are available at checkout.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**

We currently ship to the U.S., Canada, and select international destinations. Shipping rates and estimated delivery times are calculated at checkout based on your address. International orders may be subject to customs fees — the customer is responsible for any duties or import taxes.

**Q: Is shipping free?**

Free standard shipping on all U.S. orders $75 and over. Orders under $75 pay a flat rate calculated at checkout.

## Digital Products

**Q: How do I receive my digital product?**

After checkout, you'll receive an email with a download link within minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder. If you still can't find it, email support@facetea.com with your order number and we'll resend immediately.

**Q: Can I get a refund on a digital product?**

Because digital products are delivered instantly and cannot be returned, we do not offer refunds on digital downloads. However, if you have a technical issue with your download, we'll resolve it — just email us.

**Q: What format are the digital files?**

The Wealth Roadmap and Stack Challenge are PDF files. The Budget Tracker is a Google Sheets template — you'll receive a link to copy it to your own Google Drive. No software installation needed.

## Returns & Exchanges

**Q: What is your return policy?**

We accept returns on unworn, unwashed physical items within 30 days of delivery. Items must have original tags attached. Final sale items are not eligible. Free return shipping is provided for U.S. orders.

**Q: Can I exchange for a different size?**

Yes. Email support@facetea.com with your order number and the size you need. Exchanges are subject to availability. We'll confirm availability before processing.

## Products

**Q: Are your hoodies and tees print-on-demand?**

The embroidered pieces (Heavyweight Hoodie, 5-Panel Cap) are produced through our print-on-demand partner, which means slight production times are added before shipping. The graphic tees (Stack Tee, Crewneck) use screen printing. Quality is checked before every order ships.

**Q: How should I wash my apparel?**

Machine wash cold, inside out. Tumble dry low or hang dry. Avoid bleach. Washing inside out preserves graphic print life and helps maintain the vintage-wash finish on the crewneck.
