## Brand Story

Money Press started because there wasn't a brand doing both things at once — gear that looked the part *and* something real behind it.

Most streetwear is all aesthetic and nothing else. Most financial content is all function and zero style. We didn't want either.

We wanted heavyweight hoodies you wear until they fall apart. We wanted tees with graphics that mean something. And we wanted tools — spreadsheets, guides, journals — that actually help you move the needle on your money.

So we built all of it under one name.

Money Press. Like running the press. Like printing money. Like putting the work in and watching it compound.

---

## What We Sell

**Apparel.** Heavyweight hoodies, graphic tees, crewnecks, and caps. Oversized cuts, quality blanks, graphics that hold up. No fast fashion.

**Accessories.** Slim wallets, enamel pins, sticker packs. Add-ons that carry the brand wherever you go.

**Digital Tools.** Budget trackers, wealth guides, 30-day challenges. Downloadable, instant, built to actually be used.

**Journals & Planners.** The Money Press Wealth Journal is a 90-day hardcover built around stacking wins and tracking progress. No fluff, just structure.
