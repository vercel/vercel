# Build Your Wealth Wardrobe: Why What You Wear Shapes What You Do

This isn't about dressing for a paycheck you don't have yet. It's about environment design — building surroundings that remind you of who you're becoming.

The phrase "dress for success" is tired, but the underlying principle is real. Your environment shapes your behavior. What you put on in the morning is part of your environment.

## The Practical Version

We're not saying spend money on clothes to make money. We're saying be intentional about every dollar you spend — including what you wear.

A hoodie that lasts 5 years costs less per wear than a $15 fast-fashion piece that falls apart in 8 months. The per-wear math always wins. Buy heavy. Buy once.
