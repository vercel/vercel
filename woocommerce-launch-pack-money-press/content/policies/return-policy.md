# Return & Exchange Policy

**Effective Date: February 25, 2026**
**Store: Money Press**

---

## Returns — Physical Products

We accept returns on eligible physical items within **30 days of the delivery date**.

**To be eligible for a return, items must be:**
- Unworn and unwashed
- In original condition with all tags attached
- Returned in original or equivalent packaging

**Items not eligible for return:**
- Final sale or clearance items (marked clearly at time of purchase)
- Gift cards
- Digital products (see Digital Products section below)
- Items damaged due to customer misuse or improper care

---

## How to Initiate a Return

Email **support@facetea.com** with:
1. Your order number
2. The item(s) you'd like to return
3. The reason for the return

We will respond within one business day with a prepaid return shipping label (U.S. orders only) and return instructions.

**Do not send items back without contacting us first.** Returns sent without prior authorization may not be processed.

---

## Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

**Approved refunds** are processed to your original payment method within **5-10 business days**. Processing times may vary by your payment provider or bank.

**Rejected returns** (items not meeting the eligibility criteria) will be returned to you at your expense.

Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

---

## Exchanges

We offer size exchanges on apparel items subject to inventory availability. To request an exchange:

Email **support@facetea.com** with your order number and the size you need. We'll confirm availability and send instructions.

If the requested size is unavailable, we'll offer a refund or store credit.

---

## Digital Products

**All digital product sales are final.** Because digital files are delivered instantly and cannot be returned, we do not offer refunds on digital downloads (the Starter Kit, Budget Tracker, or any other digital item).

If you experience a technical issue with your download or file delivery, contact **support@facetea.com** and we will resolve it promptly — including resending files or troubleshooting access issues at no charge.

---

## Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you received a damaged item or the wrong product, contact us within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the issue and your order number. We will send a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund at your preference.
