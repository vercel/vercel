# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: February 25, 2026**
**Store: Money Press**
**Website: facetea.com**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you visit or make a purchase from Money Press, we may collect the following types of information:

**Personal Information (provided by you):**
- Name, email address, phone number
- Billing and shipping address
- Payment information (processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store full card numbers)
- Order history and purchase details
- Communications sent to our support team

**Automatically Collected Information:**
- IP address and approximate location
- Browser type and operating system
- Pages visited and time spent on site
- Referring URLs
- Device identifiers

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:**
We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, and advertising. See Section 6 for details.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- To process and fulfill your orders (physical and digital)
- To send order confirmations, shipping notifications, and delivery updates
- To respond to customer service inquiries
- To send promotional emails and product updates (with your consent — you can unsubscribe at any time)
- To improve our website, products, and customer experience
- To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized activity
- To comply with legal obligations

---

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

- **Payment processors** (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to process transactions
- **Shipping and fulfillment partners** (UPS, USPS, FedEx, Printful/Printify) to deliver your order
- **Email marketing platforms** (e.g., Klaviyo, Mailchimp) to manage communications
- **Analytics providers** (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand site performance
- **Advertising platforms** (e.g., Meta, Google) for remarketing, subject to their privacy policies
- **Legal authorities** if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our business and customers

All third-party partners are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to provide services, fulfill legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Order records are typically retained for 7 years per standard accounting and tax requirements.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction:** Request that we correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request that we delete your data (subject to legal obligations)
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in each email
- **Data Portability:** Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email **support@facetea.com**.

---

## 6. Cookies

Our website uses:
- **Essential cookies:** Required for site functionality (cart, login, checkout)
- **Analytics cookies:** To understand how visitors use the site (Google Analytics)
- **Advertising cookies:** For remarketing through Meta Pixel and Google Ads

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our store is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.
