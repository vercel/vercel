# Shipping Policy

**Effective Date: February 25, 2026**
**Store: Money Press**

---

## Order Processing

All physical orders are processed within **1-2 business days** of purchase, excluding weekends and federal holidays. If an order is placed on a weekend or holiday, processing begins the next business day.

Digital product orders are delivered **instantly** via email download link. No processing time applies.

---

## Domestic Shipping (United States)

**Standard Shipping:** 3-7 business days after processing
**Expedited Shipping:** 2-3 business days after processing (rates calculated at checkout)
**Free Shipping:** Available on all U.S. orders totaling $75 or more before tax

Shipping rates for orders under $75 are calculated at checkout based on package weight and destination.

---

## International Shipping

We ship to Canada and select international destinations. Eligibility and rates are calculated at checkout based on your shipping address.

**International delivery estimates:** 7-21 business days after processing, depending on destination and carrier.

**Customs & Duties:** International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, or taxes charged by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer. Money Press does not have control over these charges and cannot predict what they may be.

---

## Tracking

When your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. Allow up to 24 hours for tracking information to update in the carrier's system after you receive the email.

---

## Shipping Delays

Delivery estimates are not guarantees. During high-volume periods (Black Friday, holiday season, new drop launches), processing and delivery times may be extended. Weather events, carrier delays, and other factors outside our control may also affect delivery timelines.

---

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive within a reasonable timeframe after the estimated delivery window, contact us at **support@facetea.com** with your order number and we will work to resolve the issue.

---

## Contact

Questions about your shipment? Email **support@facetea.com** with your order number in the subject line. We respond within one business day.
