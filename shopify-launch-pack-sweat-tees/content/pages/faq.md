# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do these tees stop sweat completely?
No shirt can stop sweating entirely. Sweat Tee's are designed to reduce visible sweat marks and help you stay drier-looking throughout the day.

## How do your sizes fit?
Our fit is true to size with a modern athletic cut. If you prefer a looser fit, size up.

## How should I wash my tees?
Machine wash cold with similar colors. Tumble dry low or hang dry for best shape retention.

## Do you ship internationally?
Yes. We currently ship within the U.S. and selected international regions.

## How long does shipping take?
Orders are processed in 1-2 business days. Typical delivery is 3-5 business days in the U.S.

## What is your return window?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery on unworn items with original tags.

## Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes. If your item is eligible, we support easy exchanges for size changes.

## How do I contact support?
Use the contact form on our Contact page or email support@sweattees.com.
