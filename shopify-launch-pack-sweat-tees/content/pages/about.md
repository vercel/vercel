# About Sweat Tee's

Sweat Tee's was built for one reason: guys are tired of sweat marks and pit stains ruining good outfits.

We design men's tees that help reduce visible sweat marks while staying breathable, soft, and easy to wear. Every piece is made for real life—commutes, meetings, gym-adjacent days, dates, and long shifts.

## What We Stand For
- Practical confidence over hype
- Better daily essentials, not throwaway fast fashion
- Straightforward quality and fit

## Our Promise
If your tee doesn't feel right, we'll make it right with a simple return or exchange process.

## Brand Mission
To make "sweat stress" one less thing to think about.
