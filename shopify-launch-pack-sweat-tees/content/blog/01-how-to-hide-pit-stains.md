# How to Reduce Visible Pit Stains Without Overthinking Your Outfit

Pit stains are usually caused by a combo of sweat, fabric type, and movement. The easiest fix is layering smarter and choosing better fabric at the base.

## 4 Quick Tips
1. Choose sweat-resistant fabrics over thin basic cotton.
2. Wear darker core colors on high-stress days.
3. Keep a backup tee in your bag or car.
4. Wash sweat-prone tees promptly to reduce odor and discoloration.
