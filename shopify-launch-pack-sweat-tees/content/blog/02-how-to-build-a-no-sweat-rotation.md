# Build a No-Sweat Tee Rotation in 5 Minutes

A strong tee rotation removes guesswork from your week.

## Starter Rotation
- 2 dark core tees for long workdays
- 1 performance tee for active days
- 1 clean white tee for casual looks
- 1 backup tee for travel or emergencies

If you want the easiest setup, start with a 2-pack or 3-pack bundle and fill in from there.
